Twitch EventSub needs a public HTTPS endpoint. Connect gives your self-hosted recorder a public relay URL without opening router ports.

Connect forwards signed EventSub request bytes. The recorder still verifies Twitch's HMAC locally — the relay holds no Twitch credentials and no HMAC secret. For the protocol details and self-host instructions, see [Relay protocol](/docs/contributing/relay-protocol/).

## What you get

When you sign up for Connect (or self-host the relay), you get two URLs:

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Ingest URL">
    Public HTTPS URL Twitch posts EventSub deliveries to. Goes into `WEBHOOK_CALLBACK_URL` (or `PUBLIC_WEBHOOK_CALLBACK_URL` for Compose).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Subscribe URL">
    WebSocket URL the recorder dials outbound. Goes into `RELAY_SUBSCRIBE_URL`.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

## Configure

<Tabs syncKey="install-path">
  <TabItem label="Docker Compose">

  ```text
  PUBLIC_BASE_URL=https://your-replayvod.example
  PUBLIC_WEBHOOK_CALLBACK_URL=https://relay.replayvod.com/u/<token>
  RELAY_SUBSCRIBE_URL=wss://relay.replayvod.com/u/<token>/subscribe
  ```

  `PUBLIC_BASE_URL` stays your recorder's URL. `PUBLIC_WEBHOOK_CALLBACK_URL` overrides only the EventSub callback registered with Twitch — OAuth and frontend URLs are unaffected.

  </TabItem>
  <TabItem label="Manual runtime">

  ```text
  WEBHOOK_CALLBACK_URL=https://relay.replayvod.com/u/<token>
  RELAY_SUBSCRIBE_URL=wss://relay.replayvod.com/u/<token>/subscribe
  RELAY_LOCAL_CALLBACK_URL=http://127.0.0.1:8080/api/v1/webhook/callback
  ```

  `RELAY_LOCAL_CALLBACK_URL` defaults to `http://127.0.0.1:${PORT}/api/v1/webhook/callback` and only needs setting if you've moved the recorder off the default port.

  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

## Setup flow

<Steps>

1. Create or retrieve your Connect relay token.

2. Put the ingest URL into `PUBLIC_WEBHOOK_CALLBACK_URL` (Compose) or `WEBHOOK_CALLBACK_URL` (manual).

3. Put the WebSocket URL into `RELAY_SUBSCRIBE_URL`.

4. Restart ReplayVOD.

5. Confirm the logs show `Relay client started` and that EventSub subscriptions become enabled.

</Steps>

## When you don't need Connect

Skip Connect if your recorder already has a stable public HTTPS endpoint and you're comfortable exposing `/api/v1/webhook/callback` directly. See [EventSub](/docs/eventsub/) for that setup.
